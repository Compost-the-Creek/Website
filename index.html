Our Mission

We are a nonprofit organization based in Mill Creek, WA dedicated to helping local businesses reduce their carbon footprint by collecting their compost and disposing of it properly. Since 2025, we have worked with several local businesses to combat climate change by collecting over 2,000 pounds of compost, preventing over 17,000 net pounds of CO2 from entering the atmosphere.
